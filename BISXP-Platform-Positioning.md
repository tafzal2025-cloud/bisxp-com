BISXP solves the foundation once. You solve the
vertical.

TABRO.IN (India), TheUnitedSports (USA + Canada),
CareGrid (United States).

## Tone Guidelines

- Platform confidence, not consultancy humility
- Numbers and specifics over adjectives
- "Three countries. One platform." not "We work globally"
- "Production-tested" not "battle-tested" (more precise)
- Never "passionate about" or "dedicated to"
- Short sentences. Strong verbs.
- The platform speaks. Not the person.
